Identity Verification

To ensure one review per person, we need to verify your unique identity using our secure biometric simulation system.

How it works:

  • We create a unique, non-reversible identity hash
  • Your identity is checked against our database
  • You receive a secure session to submit your review
  • No personal data is stored or shared

This MVP uses browser fingerprinting (User-Agent, IP, Language) to simulate biometric identity. Production would use actual biometric authentication (fingerprint, face recognition, etc.).
