The Mycux Promise: Privacy Meets Community

Mycux is built on a foundation of cryptographic anonymity, ensuring that your identity remains private while you contribute to the community.

Anonymity-First Signup

When you sign up, your personal details (like name and email) are **immediately hashed** using a secure, one-way cryptographic function. We never store the original data. Instead, only your unique, anonymous, hashed ID is retained, making sure your **Personally Identifiable Information (PII) never touches our database** in its readable form.

Login Simulation

Logging in is a **simulation** of identity verification. You provide your credentials, we re-hash them on the fly, and compare the *new* hash against the stored anonymous ID. If they match, you're granted access. This proves your identity without ever exposing or storing your original sensitive login information.

Review Submission

Once logged in, users gain the ability to **submit honest product reviews**. Because your identity is cryptographically shielded, you can trust that your opinions are tied only to your anonymous ID, allowing for genuine, unbiased community input.
